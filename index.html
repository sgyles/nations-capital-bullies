Our Timeline

  • 2013-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We were introduced to the Alapaha Blue Blood Bulldog after attending a dog show in Birmingham,Alabama. We were completely amazed by the overall structure of the dog which in fact is what had brought us to the idea of breeding these beauties. After that moment we sat together to share our passion for this amazing breed and decided to start out own company.

  • March 2015

    We became a licensed Kennel.

    It was not easy but we did it!, this was a process that took about 12 months. With this certificate, Animal control inspects our kennel randomly. This ensures that we are a facility that is both safe for the dogs and in a very good condition. We choose to breed for working abilities, performance, temperament, and conformation. We do not accept animal cruelty and do not knowingly sell our dogs for illegal purposes.

  • December 2016

    Transition to Full Service

    After receiving the first set of pups, we acknowledged that it is our mission to satisfy people with these amazing creatures. Later, we started the breeding process and satisfied over 10 customers.

  • July 2016

    Expansion

    Starting off with only two breeders was a struggle, but we branched out to 6 breeders all over the DMV area.
